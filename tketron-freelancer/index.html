About

Hi! I'm Tyler. I'm a web developer living in the San Francisco Bay Area. I love solving challenging problems and exploring new technologies.

I developed an early curiosity and love for science and math growing up in the hills of East Tennessee. Moving to California for college, I studied environmental engineering, science, and policy and worked in several research and consulting positions before making the transition into software.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing and performing classical music, training and competing in olympic weightlifting, and preparing and enjoying various culinary treats.
